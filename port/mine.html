Minecraft Sculpture

Build it with blocks!

This project went through multiple iterations, this is the initial draft of what a latte might look like in minecraft. Because I am a novice minecraft builder I initially made the cup in a square shape to make it easier for me to build a cup.

For the final iteration of this project I was able to pair down the sides to give the cup a more sphere like appearance.
